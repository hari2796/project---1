Welcome to Coffee House

Espresso

A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It's the foundation for many other coffee drinks.

Americano

A shot of espresso diluted with hot water, resulting in a coffee similar in strength to drip coffee but with a distinct flavor.

Latte

A blend of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam. It's creamy and smooth.

Cappuccino

Equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Often topped with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon.

Macchiato

An espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam. It's stronger than a latte.
